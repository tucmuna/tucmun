Global Entertainment and Media Council

The Global Entertainment and Media Council explores key challenges shaping the future of media and entertainment. Delegates will discuss the impact of artificial intelligence on the industry, including its opportunities and ethical concerns. They will also address the media's responsibility in reporting during global crises to ensure accurate and ethical coverage.

Topic 1: The Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Media and Entertainment
Topic 2: Media Responsibility in Reporting During Global Crisis
