Sponsor: Terry Jones

Mr. Jones is thrilled to serve as the new sponsor for Tucker High School’s Model United Nations. He has spent the past five years as a Special Educator at Tucker and previously taught Social Studies. He earned a Bachelor's degree in Political Science from Old Dominion University and a Master’s in Education, specializing in Curriculum and Design, from VCU. In his free time, he enjoys playing various sports with the RVA Stonewall League, reading, and traveling. Mr. Jones looks forward to contributing to the success of TUCMUN’s inaugural conference.
